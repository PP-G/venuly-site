Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

Venuly is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not create user accounts, collect personal information, or track your activity. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Data We Collect

Venuly stores the following preferences locally on your device:

  • City or postal code selection — used to show events near you
  • Saved events — stored locally on your device only
  • Category preferences — stored locally on your device only

All of these are stored using Apple's UserDefaults and never leave your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No user accounts or sign-ups
  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or cross-app tracking

Location Data

Venuly may request access to your device's location to help you find nearby events. Location data is:

  • Only accessed when you explicitly grant permission
  • Used solely to determine your nearest city for event listings
  • Never stored on any server
  • Never shared with third parties

Cloud Services

Venuly uses Google Firestore to store and retrieve event data. The app reads event information from the database to display listings.

When you voluntarily use any of the app's submission features, the following non-personal data may be sent to our server:

  • Bug reports — your description of the issue, along with basic device information (iOS version, device model, and app version)
  • Feedback — your message, the type of feedback (bug report, feature request, or general), and basic device information (iOS version, device model, and app version)
  • Event reports — the event identifier, event title, event source, your selected reason for reporting, any additional details you provide, and basic app information (app version)

All submissions are voluntary and initiated only by you. No personal identifiers (such as name, email, or account information) are included in any of these submissions.

Third-Party Data

Event information displayed in Venuly is sourced from the Ticketmaster API. This data is displayed under Ticketmaster's terms of service. Venuly does not share any user data with Ticketmaster or any other third party.

Data Retention

Since all user preferences are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults, you have full control over your data. Uninstalling the app removes all stored preferences. Bug reports, feedback, and event reports submitted to our server are retained only for the purpose of improving the app and are not linked to any personal identity.

Children's Privacy

Venuly does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user, it is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
