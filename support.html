Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find events near me?

Open the app and select your city or enter your postal code. Venuly will show you upcoming events in your area. You can also allow location access so the app can automatically find your nearest city.

Why don't I see events in my area?

Venuly currently covers the Ottawa-Gatineau area. We're working on expanding to more cities soon.

Can I buy tickets through Venuly?

No. Venuly is an event discovery app only. When you find an event you're interested in, the app will direct you to the official ticketing source where you can purchase tickets.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Venuly works without any account or sign-up. Your preferences and saved events are stored locally on your device.

How do I save an event?

Tap the bookmark icon on any event to save it. Your saved events are stored on your device and can be accessed from the Saved tab.

What areas are supported?

Venuly currently covers events in the Ottawa-Gatineau area. More cities are coming soon.

Report a Bug

Found something that doesn't look right? We'd love to hear about it. When reporting a bug, please include:

  • A description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots, if applicable
